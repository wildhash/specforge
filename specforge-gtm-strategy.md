# SpecForge — Go-to-Market Strategy
**Version**: 1.0 | **Date**: 2026-03-02 | **Owner**: copywriter

---

## 1. Positioning Statement

**For** product builders who need to move from idea to shippable plan fast,  
**SpecForge** is a self-assembling product engine  
**that** turns a single sentence into a complete product package — PRD, architecture, GTM strategy, pitch deck, and code scaffolding — in under 5 minutes.  
**Unlike** generic AI writing tools or manual PM workflows,  
**SpecForge** deploys a coordinated team of six specialized AI agents that collaborate, validate each other's output, and build your product spec the way a real product team would — just without the meetings.

## 3. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary ICP: The Time-Constrained Builder
- **Who**: Developer, PM, or founder with a strong idea and a tight deadline
- **Context**: Hackathon, sprint kickoff, investor meeting prep, or solo founding
- **Pain**: The gap between "we have an idea" and "we have a plan" costs days they don't have
- **Trigger**: Any moment where they need professional-grade product artifacts fast
- **Success signal**: They submit one sentence and walk away with a complete product package

### Secondary ICP: The Scaling Product Team
- **Who**: PM or CPO at a startup with 5–50 engineers
- **Context**: Spec creation is a recurring bottleneck; inconsistent artifact quality slows engineering
- **Pain**: Every new feature requires the same manual documentation cycle
- **Trigger**: Third time this quarter a sprint was delayed because the spec wasn't ready
- **Success signal**: SpecForge becomes the team's default spec-generation workflow

## 4. Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Hackathon Demo (Day 0)
**Goal**: Win the hackathon. Demonstrate Complete.dev's full platform capabilities.

**Tactics**:
- Live demo: submit a real idea on stage, show the full pipeline running in real time
- Meta-narrative: "We built SpecForge using SpecForge" — show the actual artifacts generated during development
- Judges packet: complete product package (PRD, ADRs, GTM, pitch deck) generated by SpecForge itself, delivered as the submission
- Workspace walkthrough: show every agent, every artifact, every collaboration happening natively in Complete.dev

**Key message for judges**: This isn't a demo of what AI agents could do. It's a working product that does it right now.

### Phase 2: Community Launch (Week 1–2)
**Goal**: Generate organic interest among builders and founders.

**Tactics**:
- Post the "built with SpecForge" story on X/Twitter and LinkedIn — show the actual output artifacts
- Submit to Product Hunt with the meta-story as the hook
- Share in developer and PM communities (Indie Hackers, Hacker News, r/ProductManagement)
- Open the Complete.dev workspace as a public showcase — let people see the agents and artifacts

**Key message**: "We used six AI agents to build a product that uses six AI agents. Here's everything it produced."

### Phase 3: Adoption & Iteration (Month 1–3)
**Goal**: Convert interest into regular users; gather feedback for v1.1.

**Tactics**:
- Onboard first 50 users with white-glove support; document their use cases
- Build a public gallery of SpecForge-generated product packages (with permission)
- Integrate with Jira/Linear for task export (top requested feature)
- Launch domain-specific agent packs (SaaS, mobile, API products)
